# NetBeans

**Author:** Ice Liu

## Shortcuts

**Edit:**

* `Tab` Move selection right
* `Shift Tab` Move selection left
* `Ctrl Z` Undo
* `Ctrl Y` Redo

**Search:**

* `Ctrl G` Goto line
* `Ctrl F` Find
* `Ctrl H` Replace
* `Ctrl Shift F` Find in Project
* `Ctrl Shift H` Replace in Project

**Motion:**

* `Alt Left` Go back
* `Alt Right` Go forward

**Tools:**

* `Alt Enter` Spelling suggestions
